I am currently a third year PhD candidate at LAMSADE(Paris Dauphine University - PSL) in collaboration with Orange Labs.

My research topic is Heuristics Learning for Network Optimization Problem. More specifically, I'm using Monte Carlo Search and Reinforcement Learning to solve the weight setting problem and segment routing problem in telecommunication networks.

I received my master degree from Paris Dauphine University - PSL in Artificial Intelligence, Systems, Data, which is a joint PSL Master program between Paris-Dauphine, École normale supérieure, and Mines ParisTech.
